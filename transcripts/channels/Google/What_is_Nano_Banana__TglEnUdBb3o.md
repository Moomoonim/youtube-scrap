## 스크립트

I'm Naina. I'm a product manager on Nano Banana, which is Google’s image generation model. And I'm going to show you a few different ways you can use it. Nano banana is available across many Google surfaces, including Gemini App, AI mode, NotebookLM, and AI Studio, and many more that you can find in the video description. A good example of reasoning while generating is showcasing complex architectural logic. Like, design a tree house that could structurally support three adults. Nano banana will reason through what that actually means and what that requires before generating the image. When studying, you can use your textbook or your notes to create flashcards or infographics on NotebookLM. We are seeing people make ads. Small, medium businesses making menus with it. Something else that you can do is create a 3D rotating product shot. You can upload an image of your product and have Nano Banana make a 3D isometric version of it. Then you can bring it into Flow and ask it to rotate it. Another thing you can do is make a storybook with multiple images of your child's pet. You can create a customized storybook featuring your child's pet in whatever setting they want. These look great! I can't wait to share them with my nieces and nephews. Something fun I like to do is take a photo of my nephew's drawing, and ask Nano Banana to bring it to life as a 3D render. Those are just a few examples of how you can use Nano Banana. Check it out, and share what you make.
