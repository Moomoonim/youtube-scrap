## 스크립트

The All-New Nissan LEAF is the world's first third-generation EV and has a proven legacy of innovation and efficiency. Every detail has been optimized to increase the driving range to over 300 miles. Here's what makes the Nissan LEAF stand out. Flat wheels, a smooth underfloor optimized hood and bumpers, sculpted rear deck, and an active grille shutter all work together to minimize drag. A compact 3-in-1 EV powertrain integrates the motor, inverter and reducer into a single, lighter unit. Intelligent Route Planner integrates real-time traffic, battery status, and charging station locations. While Navi-Linked battery conditioning uses route data such as road gradient, speed and traffic to optimize battery temperature. The All-New Nissan LEAF engineered for efficiency.
