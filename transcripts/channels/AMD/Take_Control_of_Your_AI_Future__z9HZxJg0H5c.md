## 스크립트

AI is the most transformative technology of our time. As business leaders race to deploy AI, they are faced with a critical decision. Deploy with an open or proprietary ecosystem. Proprietary ecosystems can limit flexibility and increase long-term cost and risk. Open ecosystems allow for interoperability across a diverse set of technologies and can lower total cost of ownership. They foster rapid innovation as the entire community contributes and they enable freedom to evolve as technologies and business needs change. An open ecosystem isn't just a technology choice, it's a business strategy that puts enterprises in control.
