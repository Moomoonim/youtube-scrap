## 스크립트

This is one of those rare moments when something that once felt beyond imagination suddenly feels within reach. Quantum is our most fundamental physical theory. It's really the low-level operating system of the real physical world. For quantum computing to deliver real-world impact, it has to be both reliable and scalable. Reaching quantum at scale will allow us to take a more direct approach to solving problems in chemistry, the discovery of new material, life sciences, the way in which we bring drugs to market. It opens up millions of possibilities. Quantum will change the world, and this change is just beginning to happen.
