## 스크립트

Drivenets is a company that builds network infrastructure both for service provider and for AI builders. So anyone that builds an AI infrastructure, we are their networking solution. Drivenets enter this world of AI infrastructure and specifically AI backend networks, little shorter than three years ago and we went into a market that is, let's face it, heavily dominated by, a single player. We were looking for a GPU maker that is open, and that will allow us to be synergetic to it and together build an end-to-end architecture. With AMD, we have finally an end-to-end solution, the full stack solution, which is a) open and b) tested and validated. We started working with AMD's Instinct™ MI350 family which is the current technology, but we are already working and looking forward to implementing the AMD Helios™ Rack. When you build an AI infrastructure, typically you typically go to the specs of the GPU. But now when we're moving from a server scale to a rack scale and datacenter scale, the importance of networking becomes very clear. So when you select an infrastructure, you need to have in mind, both the compute, the storage and the networking part which connects them all. And for this again, we have this reference architecture together with AMD that includes the whole stack and make sure that your deployment will be fast and successful.
