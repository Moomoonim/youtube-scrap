## 스크립트

AMD does have a very long history of corporate responsibility, over three decades. Corporate responsibility really feeds into our purpose as a company, designing technology that tackles some of the toughest challenges in the world, and doing that in a way that really advances environmental sustainability and treats people with dignity and respect. AMD's approach to environmental sustainability is really about prioritizing where we can have the biggest impacts, and then we set goals that are time-bound and have a roadmap behind them. Investing in initiatives and strategies and programs to drive those roadmaps is a key element of progress. Finally, we report on our progress and our journey every year through transparent, clear disclosures. The biggest opportunity for the environment from AMD's perspective is really to increase renewable energy use. We have been able to expand our business operations globally while slashing carbon emissions. We've gone from focusing on our own operations to really leading on product energy efficiency. To see that all come together has been really exciting, and we go further in the sense that we shape whole industry dialogues on this. We help to set standards and understand global regulations and frameworks. So in that sense, AMD plays an outsized role in advancing sustainability across the industry. I love my job. It is my dream job. And I feel very grateful to be such part of an important role in not only our own company's sustainability journey, but in the broader industry and our overarching trajectory of providing leading, high-performance technology in a more sustainable manner.
