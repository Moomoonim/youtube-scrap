## 스크립트

The apps you build an AI Studio deserve to be shared with the world. Let's take a look at a quick example to see how this is done. So in this case, we have a multiplayer racing game I've created, and I want to publish it to a URL so I can play against my friends and share it with the world. To get started with publishing your app, all you need to do is click the publish button in the top right. And if you're a new user to AI Studio and to Google Cloud, we'll go ahead and set everything up for you. So it's just a few clicks to get started. Or if you have an existing Google Cloud project, you can bring that to publish to as well. Once you add a few details about your app, you can click publish and you'll have a live URL ready to share with friends. Your app will always be defaulted to the free tier of Google Cloud Run, so there's no cost to you unless you go beyond those free thresholds. And if you need inspiration for what to build next, go ahead and check out the app gallery in AI Studio, where we have a ton of different app examples that you can also remix to add your own twist. Once you've gone through this flow and published your app, drop a link in the comments to your app. We'd love to see what you're building.
