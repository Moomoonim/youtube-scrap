## 스크립트

we truly believe that the most important aspect of an employee when they walk through the door is do they feel like they're attached to the work? Do they feel like they're attached to the purpose of the organization? When you see the light in people come on because they can be their authentic selves at work and they can contribute mightily to the work that they're doing, it's the best feeling in the world. We want to make sure that in every room where a decision is made, we have multiple voices from the orchestra contributing to that decision. we're constantly looking for what is the added voice that each person brings that doesn't already exist in the organization. And we embed that in our development programs and ensuring that managers understand how to let people be their authentic selves, how to get unique perspectives and everything that we believe about innovation, stems from having as many voices as possible that contribute to a positive outcome. I want to see people achieve beyond what they think their personal best is. providing people the space and in a place where they can be themselves, they can achieve far more. And I get to be that conduit to help them do that. And that thrills me.
