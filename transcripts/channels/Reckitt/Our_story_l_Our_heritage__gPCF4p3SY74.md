## 스크립트

At Wreckit, we are incredibly proud of our long-standing heritage, a history full of stories of the people who work here, the communities we serve, and our power brands that have made us the worldclass consumer health and hygiene company we are today. It all started in 1840 when Isaac and Thomas Racket leased a factory in Hull, Yorkshire, UK. Fun fact, their first product was laundry starch. As a company, Wreckit continued to grow, developing products and investing in communities. [music] Over the years, we've built a family of iconic brands that have been protecting, healing, and nurturing our consumers and communities for centuries. Did you know in the [music] 1930s that Dettle helped reduce maternal fatalities from sepsis by 50%. or that Lysol supported the eradication of Spanish influenza, a pandemic that had killed tens of millions of people by 1920. And Harpik became our first brand to be advertised on TV. &gt;&gt; Today, we can proudly look back on nearly 200 years of science and innovation across our brands. Drawing on this rich experience will help us meet the challenges of today and help us chart the best course for our future. Continuing to tell our story and write our heritage together.
