We're heading to one of the most dense and unpredictable driving environments, New York. Starting from the Great Lakes in Chicago, traveling through Detroit's motor city and navigating the maze of bridges in Pittsburgh, this presents a brand new set of challenges for our end-to-end driving model. From wide interstates to dense urban grids, our AI makes intelligent decisions wherever the road takes us. At every stage, the Wave AI driver demonstrates highly performant driving without prior training. This road show is another step towards making autonomous driving A reality for everyone everywhere.
