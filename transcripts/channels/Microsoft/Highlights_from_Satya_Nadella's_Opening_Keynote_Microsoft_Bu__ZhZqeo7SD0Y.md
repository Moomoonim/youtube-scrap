If there's one key takeaway, it would be this. How do you all participate fully in this frontier intelligence ecosystem? In order to really push the limits, realize this unmetered intelligence, what if we could just max the compute, max the memory, build out that developer machine that's the dream machine? And that's what we're announcing today. Surface RTX Spark Dev Box. And so this idea that the PC evolved from a personal computer to a personal AI, it's just really exciting. We are building a new intelligence layer, bringing together the models, context as well as the tools. This model is coupled with live telemetry, so it reflects the real operational state of the grid minute by minute. And today, we are really thrilled to announce that OpenClaw runs on Windows leveraging MXC. Watching a Claw try to delete all your desktop files and just fail makes me really happy. We are introducing Autopilots. These are autonomous, long-running agents with full enterprise compliance that run in your tenant. And so this is Majorana 2. Majorana 2 provides qubit mean lifetime of 20 seconds or up to even a minute, essentially a thousand times higher than what we were able to achieve with Majorana 1. It's about tackling those pressing challenges of people and planet. That's our North Star for the frontier ecosystem. Let's all go build together. Thank you all very, very much.
