## 스크립트

The All-New Nissan LEAF is the world's first third-generation EV with over 28 billion kilometers driven and 700,000 units sold. The Nissan LEAF has a proven legacy of safety and innovation. Here's what makes the Nissan LEAF stand out. Safety shield 360 A comprehensive system of cameras, radar and sonar which monitor in front, besides and behind the vehicle as it drives. Intelligent 3D Around View Monitor offers a virtual bird's eye view of the car for easier parking and maneuvering. Front Wide View expands visibility around corners and past parked cars. Invisibile Hood View projects a virtual image through the engine bay to help spot curbs and other low obstacles. The CMF-EV platform provides protection for both the battery and passengers during collisions ensuring peace of mind on every journey. The All-New Nissan LEAF engineered for safety.
