## 스크립트

The car you drive. The phone in your pocket. The plane overhead. Today, products like these are shaped long before they're built. Through simulation. Before a single part is built, engineers ask the same question again and again. Will it work? Change an angle. Swap a material. Reshape a curve. Each idea, a question. Each question, a simulation. But simulations take time. And while the machine runs, the momentum fades. Designing becomes waiting. Ansys Discovery was built to close that gap. To help engineers see how heat moves and fluids flow while the design is still fresh in their minds. Work like this used to demand more than a workstation could easily provide. Big models. Expensive hardware. Remote servers. Hours of prep before a single result. Meanwhile, product design keeps getting more complex. And the pressure to move faster keeps climbing. So Ansys made advanced simulation part of the local design workflow. With AMD Ryzen AI Max+ PRO processors, Ansys Discovery can run complex thermal and fluid simulations directly on compact workstations. Helping engineers test ideas without leaving the environment where those ideas are taking shape. Less queuing. Less handoff. Less waiting. A question becomes a simulation. A simulation becomes an insight. An insight becomes the next move. Simulations that once took hours, now finish in minutes. That is not just speed. That is more ideas tested. More options explored. More confidence before a single part is built. Will it work? Now the engineer can find out while the idea is still fresh. That is what Ansys and AMD are helping make possible. Simulation that keeps up with the pace of innovation.
