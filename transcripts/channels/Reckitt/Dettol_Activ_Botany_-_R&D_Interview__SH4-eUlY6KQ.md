## 스크립트

Dettol is is an iconic brand. It's the symbol of germ protection, and it's also extra special because it came from home nearly 100 years ago. &gt;&gt; Dettol was actually first created and manufactured in Hull, which is my hometown, so it makes me really proud. I often get a lot of people talking about how their grandparents used to bathe them in Dettol ISL brown, and it's really nice to see that a lot of people have this memory. &gt;&gt; Dettol Active Botany is a new generation of disinfectant, providing 99.999% germ kill with plant-based actives, amazing fragrances infused with with essential oils. It's suitable for the whole family, including pets, children, and babies. You can use it for your laundry and all your hard surfaces in your house. Incredibly versatile, and leaves your house smelling great. We selected two germ kill actives that are plant-based, benzalkonium chloride and citric acid, and together they have synergistic effect. Citric acid works as an antimicrobial booster for for BKC, so it traps the metal ions that are present in the water to allow benzalkonium chloride to perform at its best. &gt;&gt; The launch in China has been really unique because there was nothing like it in the market. The feedback and the response has been really strong. Consumers have absolutely embraced the product, and it just goes to show as well that the there was a real gap in the market, and that this product has filled that gap. When you see pictures online, and when we get small glimpses of social media influencers using it, and feedback from our marketeering team, it just kind of blows your mind a little bit. Um but it makes me feel really proud, you know, I've shared it all with my family. Whenever I can put it on LinkedIn, it's really cool to to share with my peers as well. &gt;&gt; Launching a Dettol product is a collaboration between different teams and it's it's a global communication, not only here in Hull, but with China, with the factory, marketing, regulatory, safety, quality. Everybody wants this to to succeed and there's a real passion to to make sure that we deliver great products on the market.
