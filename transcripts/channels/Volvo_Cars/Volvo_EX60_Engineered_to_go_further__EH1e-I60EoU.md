## 스크립트

With a range of up to 810 km on a single charge, the EX60 is optimized for efficiency. And it all begins here in Goththingberg. [music] For the EX60, we completely change how we build our cars, refining and optimizing every stage of production. &gt;&gt; [music] &gt;&gt; With Mega Casting, we use high pressure aluminum casting to form large body sections [music] in a single operation. Traditionally, one section of a car could mean a 100 separate parts, each with its own supply chain, tooling, and waste. But with the EX60, all of that complexity [music] is replaced by this one single component. making the whole car lighter, simpler, and more efficient. &gt;&gt; But Mega Casting [music] is just the beginning. The EX60 introduces celltobody technology, another leap forward in how we build our cars. &gt;&gt; Unlike typical EVs with heavy bulky battery packs, the EX60 battery cells are integrated directly into the body of the vehicle. Essentially, the battery pack becomes the floor of the vehicle, which gives great advantages in weight and packaging efficiency, [music] boosting boat range and performance. It also opens up more cabin space. [music] Crucially, both Mega Casting and Seltobody technology help to reduce weight, and that means more range for the car. These integrations are just two [music] examples of our relentless optimization across every system in the EX60, which enables [music] us to offer our most efficient and fastest charging fully electric Volvo yet.
