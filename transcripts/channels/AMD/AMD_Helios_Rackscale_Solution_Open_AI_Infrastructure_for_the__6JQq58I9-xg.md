## 스크립트

One of the unique attributes of AI adoption compared to previous large technology transitions is the speed at which it's being adopted. Leading AI model providers have quickly scaled to support more than a billion active users, and this adoption continues to accelerate globally. Rackscale solutions are the new infrastructure building block to allow this scaling to efficiently support frontier models at gigawatt data center scale by tightly coupling the entire rack of GPU compute into a high bandwidth scale-up pod, which improves performance, reliability, and availability. AMD enters this moment with real market momentum. AMD Instinct GPUs are being adopted by AI leaders with major deployments announced by OpenAI, Meta, Microsoft, and Oracle. AMD continues to partner with large government and sovereign initiatives such as the US Genesis Mission and the EuroHPC Joint Undertaking, and many more. Together, these commitments reinforce where the market is moving towards open, scalable AI infrastructure built for the next era of AI and sovereign deployments. And they set the stage for AMD's next big step, the AMD Helios Rackscale solution powered by AMD's Instinct MI455X GPUs. Helios is an open, co-designed Rackscale AI platform built for the AI factory era. At the heart of Helios are 72 AMD Instinct MI455X GPUs, delivering leadership compute of up to 2.9 exaFLOPS of FP4 compute, up to 1.4 exaFLOPS of FP8 compute, and up to 31 terabytes of HBM4 memory, while setting a new bar for memory bandwidth at the rack level. That combination of memory capacity and memory bandwidth is critical for the next phase of AI. Developed jointly by Meta and AMD through the Open Compute Project, AMD Helios brings open standards to rack-level AI infrastructure. The Helios open rack-wide architecture aligns rack infrastructure, power, cooling, and serviceability to support scalable AI factories for frontier model development and high-throughput inference. Inside the rack, UALink over Ethernet provides fast scale-up across all 72 GPUs. Across racks, standards-based Ethernet scale-out connects Helios into larger clusters while preserving openness, flexibility, and ecosystem choice. Helios brings together AMD Instinct MI455X GPUs, AMD EPYC Venice CPUs, AMD Pensando Networking, and AMD ROCm software into one integrated rack-scale solution, designed to power high-throughput inference, frontier model training, fine-tuning, agentic AI, sovereign AI, and the next generation of AI applications. With Helios, AMD gives partners the foundation to build AI factories, delivering both leadership performance and compelling TCO, using open, scalable, and secure infrastructure for the next era of AI.
