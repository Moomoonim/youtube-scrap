## 스크립트

The AMD Internship Program is extremely important because it's the way that we hire a significant proportion of the engineers into this company. They get to work with extremely skilled engineers and work on advanced technologies and products. The major thing that surprised me about working in AMD was how you're trusted to work independently. We have a very collaborative culture here at AMD where you're encouraged to ask questions and if you're stuck there's always going to be someone to help you. My first impression of the team was very supportive and they took their time to guide me and help me succeed. They weren't strictly looking for my technical knowledge but they were really interested in my ability to solve problems and think through things critically. The interview process wasn't as scary as I was expecting it to be. They just wanted to see where my knowledge stands and whether or not I think I'd be able to be able to solve problems. When I was actually doing the technical interview there were some questions that I wasn't 100% about. Some of the team were actually really helpful and talked me through how to actually solve the problem that I was given. Working at AMD has allowed me to develop a wide set of skills whether it be using the latest AI tools, my technical knowledge in circuit design or even more soft skills like presentation and communication as well. Working at AMD has really advanced my career because it is a market leader in the semiconductor space. I've been able to work on a wide breadth of projects both from the software as well as the hardware side. AMD technology has been surrounding me my whole life and I just really want to be part of that innovation.
