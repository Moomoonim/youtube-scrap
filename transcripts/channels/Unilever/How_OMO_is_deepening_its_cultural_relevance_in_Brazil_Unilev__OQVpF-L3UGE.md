## 스크립트

How is Omo growing its relevance in Brazil? By embedding itself in culture through its Dirt Is Glory campaign, co-created with Omo, Arsenal, and Conzilia. The campaign taps into the spirit of Brazilian Varzia grassroots football and follows eight teams competing for the chance to bring their story to Emirates Stadium in the Omo Varziano Cup. Dirt Is Glory Is Omo's most far-reaching culture-led and social-first campaign, reaching 120 million people with 70-plus pieces of coverage in Brazil and the UK, all of which is building desire at scale for our largest laundry brand.
