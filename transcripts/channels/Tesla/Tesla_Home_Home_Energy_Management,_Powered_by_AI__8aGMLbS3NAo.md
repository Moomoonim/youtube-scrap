## 스크립트

Tesla home is your home energy management system that can help you save money on your electricity costs by automatically adjusting how energy is used to power your home. Tesla home is powered by Opticaster, our AI optimization software. It assesses your energy usage and the weather forecast to predict your home's energy needs and then decides where to source your energy from and how best to use it in your home. This allows your home to automatically use solar or stored energy when utility prices are high while your Powerwall and electric vehicle charge from solar or the grid when prices are low, helping to minimize your electricity costs. It comes standard with every Powerwall and seamlessly connects Powerwall to solar panels, solar roof, wall connector, and other third-party energy products, no additional equipment needed. Monitor everything in the Tesla app to see what your system is doing, what it plans to do next, and most importantly, why. Just set your energy goals and Tesla home will take care of the rest.
