I have a very strong sense of identity in my blindness. One of the misconceptions that people have about blind people is that we don't care about how we look or the way we present ourselves to the world. The Ray-Ban Meta Glasses allows me to still look fashionable and be able to do the tasks that I need done as a blind person. - Hey, Meta call a volunteer with Be My Eyes. - Calling a volunteer. - Hey there. Try that. - This one here? - Yeah, that's it. - Perfect. Thank you. - Being able to connect with a human through the Be My Eyes Call feature makes a world of difference. - How can I help? - Hi, I'm going to a salsa night later today... - I'm able to ask follow-up questions and get more detailed information. - Try the next one. Found it. - The benefits to using Be My Eyes through the glasses is being able to be hands-free. As somebody that uses a cane, I love being independent. That makes a huge difference. - Hi, I was wondering if you could just give me a spot check to see if I missed anything. - Yes, I'm taking a look. You look amazing. It looks perfect. - Oh, thank you so much. - I'm already like a very active, extroverted person and I feel like the glasses help me be able to be that even more. I think the Ray-Ban Meta Glasses will make a huge difference in people being able to use the tools that they need to live the lives that they want to live.
