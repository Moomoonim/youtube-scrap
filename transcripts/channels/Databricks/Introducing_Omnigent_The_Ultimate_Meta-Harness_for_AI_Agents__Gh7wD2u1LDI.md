## 스크립트

Agent harnesses have become incredibly powerful, but they exist in silos. [music] And as project scale, it becomes difficult to coordinate multiple agents across the same workflow. So, we built Omnigent, a meta harness that sits above your agents and provides composition, control, and collaboration. Omnigent gives you one unified interface where you can combine multiple models, harnesses, and techniques for building robust multi-agent workflows. Seamlessly switch between Auto Code, CodeX, Pi, [music] and your own agents with just one line change. Omnigent introduces stateful data-centric policies that track agent actions dynamically and enforce guardrails like cost budgets and access controls. [music] You can also use Omnigent in a secure sandbox that runs in an isolated remote container. And when it comes to collaboration, Omnigent lets you share live agent sessions via a URL, &gt;&gt; [music] &gt;&gt; so your teammates can review, comment, and steer your agent together in real time. And with Omnigent, you can collaborate from anywhere, whether it's your terminal, browser, desktop, or even your phone. Models and harnesses will continue to change, but the layer you work at shouldn't have to. Omnigent is open source and available now. Start orchestrating your agents today at [music] omnigent.ai.
