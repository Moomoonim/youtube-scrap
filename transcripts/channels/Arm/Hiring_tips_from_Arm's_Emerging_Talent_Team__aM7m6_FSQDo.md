## 스크립트

To stand out in our interview process, it's really important that we see the real authentic you. We want to know what you enjoy doing, what your passions are, your technical strengths and skills and understanding. So, it's really important that our graduates are comfortable with that changing landscape and that ambiguity at times. One thing that candidates shouldn't worry about is the friendliness and the approachableness of our interviewers. They want to make sure that you are comfortable in an interview, you're relaxed. They want you to do your best. And to do that is for them to make sure that they're welcoming and that that you feel at your very best during the interview. So, um you will get lots of support throughout the process. Um we have our emerging talent recruiters and our coordinators who'll be there to guide you through every step of the process, to ask questions, and ensure that you are comfortable and confident throughout the process.
